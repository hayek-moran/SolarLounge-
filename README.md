# ☀️ Solar Lounge: Residential Microgrid Design & Feasibility Study 

## 🎓 Project Overview
**Author:** Moran Hayek | Computer Engineering Student at the **Technion - Israel Institute of Technology** 🏛️  
**Location:** Northern District, Israel 📍  
**Simulation Date:** March-April, 2026  
**Status:** Detailed Design & Engineering Specification Phase (Optimized) 🏗️

This project represents a high-premium architectural and engineering transformation of a 96m² residential rooftop. By integrating an **oversized** photovoltaic array with optimized smart energy storage, the design achieves superior energy independence and creates a luxury "Off-Grid" living environment. 🔋✨

---

## ⚡ Engineering Specifications
The system is designed as a high-availability microgrid, leveraging high-efficiency bifacial technology and a strategic DC/AC oversizing approach:

* **Generation Capacity**: **13.6 kWp** total nominal power.
* **PV Modules**: **20 units** of **Trina Vertex N 680W** Bifacial modules.
* **String Configuration**: Optimized into **2 strings of 10 modules** in series to maintain safe voltage thresholds.
* **Inverter**: **10 kWac** 3-Phase Hybrid Inverter (**Solis S6-EH3P10K2-H**).
* **DC/AC Ratio**: **1.36 Pnom ratio**, designed to maximize energy harvesting during morning, evening, and winter months.
* **Energy Storage**: **14.3 kWh** LFP (LiFePO4) Battery Bank (**10.8 kWh usable**) configured at **154V**.
* **System Logic**: Self-consumption strategy with max charging power set to **7.0 kW**.

---

## 📊 System Performance & ROI
Based on the latest PVSyst simulation results, the "Solar Lounge" sets a new benchmark for residential energy performance:

* **Annual Production**: **23,443 kWh/year**.
* **Solar Fraction (SF)**: **56.63%** direct energy coverage from renewable sources.
* **Specific Production**: **1,724 kWh/kWp/year**.
* **Performance Ratio (PR)**: **78.87%** (accounting for realistic Israeli thermal losses).
* **Sustainability Impact**: Estimated **419.2 tons of CO2 saved** over a 30-year lifecycle.

## 🏗️ Architectural Synthesis
Beyond the electrical engineering, the project focuses on **Space Optimization**. By removing obsolete rooftop equipment and utilizing high-efficiency thermal technology (Centralized Heat Pump), the design reclaims over **50m²** of living space, transforming a utility area into a premium lounge that enjoys the northern breeze. 🌬️🍹
