# ☀️ Solar Lounge 96: Residential Microgrid Design & Feasibility Study

## 🎓 Project Overview
**Author:** Moran Hayek | Computer Engineering Student at the **Technion - Israel Institute of Technology** 🏛️  
**Location:** North District, Israel 📍  
**Status:** Detailed Design & Engineering Specification Phase 🏗️  

This project represents a high-premium architectural and engineering transformation of a 96m^2 residential rooftop. By integrating advanced photovoltaic hardware with smart energy storage, the design achieves total energy independence and creates a luxury "Off-Grid" living environment. 🔋✨

---

## ⚡ Engineering Specifications
The system is designed as a high-availability microgrid with the following core components:

* **Generation Capacity:** 10.2kWp via 15 times Trina Vertex N 680W Bifacial modules. ☀️
* **Energy Storage:** 22kWh LFP (LiFePO_4) Battery Bank configured in modular blocks. 🔋
* **System Brain:** 10kW 3-Phase Hybrid Inverter with integrated Automatic Transfer Switch (ATS). 🧠
* **Energy Coverage:** 107% annual autonomy (approx 17,340kWh/year). 📈
* **Thermodynamic Upgrade:** Centralized 300\% efficiency Heat Pump replacing traditional solar collectors. 🌡️

## 🏗️ Architectural Synthesis
Beyond the electrical engineering, the project focuses on **Space Optimization**. By removing obsolete rooftop equipment and utilizing high-efficiency thermal technology, the design reclaims over $50m^2$ of living space, transforming a utility area into a premium lounge that enjoys the northern breeze. 🌬️🍹
